Mango Crop Diseases and Preventive Measures

Disease Preventive Measures
Anthracnose 1. Prune and destroy infected plant parts.
2. Use disease-free seeds or seedlings.
3. Apply fungicides as per recommendations.
Powdery Mildew 1. Maintain proper spacing between plants.
2. Ensure good air circulation.
3. Apply sulfur-based fungicides.
Black Spot 1. Prune affected parts and destroy.
2. Maintain orchard hygiene.
3. Apply copper-based fungicides.
Root Rot 1. Ensure well-drained soil.
2. Avoid over-watering.
3. Rotate crops and avoid planting in infected areas.
